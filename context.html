Historical Background

The Iranian Revolution of 1979 serves as the central historical event in Persepolis. This revolution transformed Iran from a monarchy under Shah Mohammad Reza Pahlavi to an Islamic Republic under Ayatollah Khomeini, dramatically altering social and political structures.

Key Events

  • The 1953 CIA-backed coup against Prime Minister Mossadegh
  • The White Revolution modernization efforts (1963)
  • The Islamic Revolution (1978-1979)
  • The Iran-Iraq War (1980-1988)

Cultural Shifts

Following the revolution, Iran experienced significant cultural changes including:

  • Mandatory veiling laws for women
  • Restrictions on Western cultural influences
  • Changes in educational curriculum
  • Censorship of media and arts
