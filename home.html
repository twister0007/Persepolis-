About the Book

Persepolis is a graphic novel by Marjane Satrapi that depicts her childhood and early adult years in Iran during and after the Islamic Revolution. The book provides a unique perspective on the impact of political and social upheaval on ordinary people.

About the Author

Marjane Satrapi is an Iranian-born French graphic novelist, cartoonist, illustrator, film director, and children's book author. Her work often explores themes of identity, culture, and the human experience.
